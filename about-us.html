About SupplementInsider

📅 Last Updated: April 16, 2026  |  Founded: 2024

SupplementInsider is an independent research and review platform dedicated to providing honest, evidence-based analysis of health and wellness products. We exist to help everyday consumers make informed decisions in a marketplace crowded with bold claims and conflicting information.

Our Mission

The dietary supplement industry is worth over $150 billion globally, yet most consumers struggle to separate effective products from marketing hype. Our mission is to bridge that gap through clear, thorough, and unbiased product analysis.

Every review we publish is built on three pillars:

1. Ingredient Research — We examine each active ingredient against published scientific literature, traditional use evidence, and clinical study findings.
2. Product Transparency — We assess manufacturing standards, third-party testing, ingredient sourcing, and label accuracy.
3. Real Customer Insights — We aggregate and analyze customer feedback from multiple sources to identify consistent patterns of experience.

Our Editorial Standards

Independence

We maintain strict editorial independence. While we participate in affiliate programs that help fund our work, no brand or manufacturer pays us for positive coverage. Our reviews reflect honest analysis based on research, not commercial relationships.

Transparency

Every article clearly discloses affiliate relationships when present. We separate factual product information from opinion-based assessment. When evidence is limited or mixed, we say so directly.

Accuracy

We cite primary sources where possible. When we make claims about ingredient effects, we ground them in published research. Errors are corrected promptly when identified, and significant updates are noted at the top of revised articles.

Currency

Health science evolves. We review and update our most-read articles at least every 6 months to reflect new research, updated product formulations, and changing market conditions.
