Affiliate Disclosure

📅 Last Updated: April 16, 2026
FTC Compliance Notice: SupplementInsider participates in affiliate marketing programs. When you click outbound product links on our site and complete a purchase, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you.

What This Means

SupplementInsider is a free resource for consumers researching health supplements. Our content is funded primarily through affiliate marketing partnerships. This means:

  • Some links on our site direct readers to product pages on third-party websites
  • If you click these links and complete a purchase, we may receive a small commission
  • The commission comes from the merchant's marketing budget, not from your wallet
  • You pay the same price whether you use our links or visit the merchant directly

Our Editorial Independence

We want to be absolutely clear: our affiliate relationships do not influence our editorial content. Specifically:

  • We do not accept payment for positive reviews
  • Our research methodology is the same for products with and without affiliate programs
  • We share both positive and negative findings honestly
  • We will not recommend a product we do not believe has merit, regardless of commission rates
  • Editorial decisions are made by our content team, separate from any business relationships

Why We Use Affiliate Marketing

Producing thorough, well-researched supplement reviews takes significant time and resources. We invest in:

  • Research staff and medical reviewers
  • Access to scientific literature and databases
  • Website hosting and technology
  • Content production and editing

Affiliate commissions allow us to keep all our content free for readers while maintaining our quality standards. Without this funding model, we would either need to charge subscription fees or accept advertising that could compromise editorial independence.

How to Identify Affiliate Links

Throughout our content, you may encounter outbound links to product manufacturers, retailers, or affiliate networks. These links typically appear as:

  • "Order Now" or "Buy Here" call-to-action buttons
  • Product name links within review text
  • "Visit Official Website" links in pricing sections

Most outbound product links on our site are affiliate links. When you click one, you may notice a redirect or tracking parameter in the destination URL.

Our Affiliate Partners

We work with various affiliate networks and direct partnerships, including but not limited to:

  • ClickBank
  • BuyGoods
  • Digistore24
  • Direct manufacturer programs
  • Other reputable affiliate networks in the health and wellness space

Your Choice as a Reader

You are never obligated to use our affiliate links. If you prefer, you can:

  • Visit product manufacturer websites directly
  • Search for products independently
  • Compare prices across multiple retailers
  • Use our content as research reference only

However, when you do choose to use our links, you support our continued ability to produce free, in-depth supplement research.

Honest Recommendations Policy

Our reviews include both products we recommend and those we do not. When we identify concerns about a product, including:

  • Insufficient ingredient transparency
  • Lack of clinical evidence
  • Pricing concerns
  • Customer complaint patterns
  • Manufacturer credibility issues

We share these findings openly, even if it means losing potential commission revenue. Our long-term value to readers depends on this honesty.

Questions or Concerns

If you have any questions about our affiliate relationships, advertising practices, or editorial integrity, please reach out through our Contact page. We are committed to full transparency.

Legal Compliance

This disclosure complies with the U.S. Federal Trade Commission's "Guides Concerning the Use of Endorsements and Testimonials in Advertising" (16 CFR Part 255) and applicable international advertising standards.
